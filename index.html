Who are we?

The Mbondo tribe, also known as the Mbembe and the Babembe, lives in predominantly in the Democratic Republic of the Congo DRC. It is essential to note that Africa is home to numerousethnic groups, each with its own culture, history, and traditions. The Bembe tribe is just one of the many generous and intriguing ethnic groups that contribute to the cultural richness of the African continent.

What is our mission?

Helping the elderly and young children who can't help themselves on the street and in their homes. Collaborate with existing organisations and local authorities to ensure our efforts align with the community's requirements and to maximise our impact. Together,we can construct a society that becomes more inclusive and compassionate towards those in need.

How do you accomplish our mission?

Advocate for enhanced social services and support systems for the elderly and vulnerable children with local authorities and community leaders. By increasing awareness of the obstacles they confront. influence policies and resources that effectively address their needs. Provide practical assistance to those in need, such as food, clothing,and shelter. In addition, you can provide assistance with domestic chores, repairs, or simply companionship and emotional support.
