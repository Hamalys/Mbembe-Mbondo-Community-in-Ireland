We work with local officials and community leaders to improve social services and support systems for the elderly and children who are at risk. By making more people aware of the problems they face, we want to change policies and get tools that meet their needs well. We help people in need with things like food, clothes, and a place to live. We can also help with housework, repairs, or just be there for someone to talk to and offer mental support.

Organizations Tree

Board of Directors

As the organization's leaders and chief decision-makers, the Board of Directors makes important decisions about policies and strategies. Watching over the organization's activities, deciding its long-term goals, and making sure its purpose and objectives are met are all their job. Regular board meetings are held to make important choices, give advice to the senior team, and make sure the organisation works in an open and responsible way.

Chairman

The Chairman of the Board is a very important leadership position. They are in charge of running board meetings and making sure the board works well. They are in charge of the board and work together with the CEO and other board members. Setting the organization's strategic direction and keeping an eye on government issues are very important jobs of the Chairman.

CEO/Executive Director

The CEO or Executive Director oversees managing and directing the entire organization. Together with the board, they create and implement long-term plans, oversee day-to-day operations, and ensure the organization's mission and objectives are achieved and maintained.

Management Team

The Management Team works with the CEO to ensure the organization runs smoothly and comes up with plans for how to reach our goal. They have many groups that work together to make sure everything runs smoothly.

Programs/Services Department

To meet the needs of people all over Ireland, the Programs/Services Department plans, carries out, and evaluates programs and services. To do this, they have to figure out what the community needs, make programs that meet those needs, and then figure out how well those programs worked.

Program Manager

While working with teams to make sure the programs are carried out successfully, program managers are in charge of those programs. The program leaders decide what the goals are, how to use the resources available, and how well the program is doing.

Program Coordinator

Coordinators of programs help Programme Managers run and coordinate programs all over Ireland. They work together with partners, staff, and volunteers to make sure the program is a success.

Support Staff

In our organization, the Support Staff helps different areas with both operational and administrative tasks. They are in charge of things like managing data, making schedules, and keeping the office in good shape.

Finance

The Finance department is in charge of our company's money and makes sure it can stay in business. Their job is to make budgets, do accounts, report on finances, and raise money from the government, our members, and charitable individuals everywhere.

Human Resources

Concerns about employees are handled by the Human Resources department. In this role, they are responsible for hiring people, teaching them, managing their work, and making sure they follow HR rules and regulations regarding labor.

Volunteers

An overwhelming majority of our Volunteers give their time and skills to help the group's events and programs. In addition to helping staff and beneficiaries, they may act as teachers, assistants, or advocates.

Beneficiaries/Vulnerable Individuals

Most of the people who benefit from this help are Mmbondo communities, which live in all of Ireland's counties, as well as other minority ethnic groups that live in Ireland and the communities that the organization helps by offering a range of support services. These services could include getting an education, medical care, a place to live, food, and help with community growth to make their lives better and improve their health.
